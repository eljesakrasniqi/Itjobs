Computer analyst

What is an "Computer analyst"?

Analysts create diagrams to help programmers and architects build computer systems. Computer systems analysts, sometimes called systems architects, study an organization's current computer systems and procedures and design improvements to them. In doing so, these analysts help the organization operate more efficiently.

System analyst is a good position for those with interest in computer science, information technology, and management. However, the career often consists of long hours and high-stress situations. You work closely with a team to efficiently solve problems with the computer systems of large organizations.
