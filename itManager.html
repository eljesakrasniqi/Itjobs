What is an "IT manager"?

An IT Manager is a professional who ensures that all employees have the technology they need to get their job done, from a reliable laptop and VPN access for remote workers who can't be reached otherwise, to up-close coordination with other departments like HR or finance so data is secure.Information Technology Managers rank #4 in Best Technology Jobs. Running regular checks on network and data security. Identifying and acting on opportunities to improve and update software and systems. Developing and implementing IT policy and best practice guides for the organisation.
