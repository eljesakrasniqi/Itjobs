What is Information Technology (IT)?

What is IT?

Information technology (IT) is the use of any computers, storage, networking and other physical devices, infrastructure and processes to create, process, store, secure and exchange all forms of electronic data.Typically, IT is used in the context of business operations, as opposed to technology used for personal or entertainment purposes.

What is a career in IT?

Careers in IT involve the design, development, support and management of hardware, software, multimedia and systems integration services. The information technology industry is a dynamic and entrepreneurial field that continues to have a revolutionary impact on the economy and on the world.

What is the role of IT?

Information technology facilitates storing and regaining huge information quickly with the help of hardware and software networks and workstations at lesser costs. Information technology enables combination and configuration of data to create distinctly new information which aids in making quick decisions.
